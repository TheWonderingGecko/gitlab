Education Background

During my time at UMKC pursuing a CS degree, I had an incredible experience that left a lasting impact on my academic and personal growth. The professors at UMKC were not only highly knowledgeable in their fields but also incredibly insightful. Their passion for computer science was contagious, and they consistently provided valuable perspectives that challenged and expanded my understanding of the subject matter. Overall, my time at UMKC was an enriching journey that equipped me with the necessary skills, insights, and network to thrive in the ever-evolving field of computer science.
